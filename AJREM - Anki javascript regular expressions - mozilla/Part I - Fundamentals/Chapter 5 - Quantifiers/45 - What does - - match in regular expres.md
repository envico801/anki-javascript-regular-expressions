========== Question ==========  

### What does `/<.*>/` match in regular expressions?  

========== Answer ==========  

Greedily matches everything between the first '_<_' and the last '_>_' in a string.

**Example**: `/<.*>/` matches "_`<foo><bar>`_" in "_start `<foo><bar>` end_".
