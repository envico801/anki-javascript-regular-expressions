========== Question ==========  

### What does `x{n}` match in a regular expression?  

========== Answer ==========  

**Matches exactly '_n_' occurrences of '_x_'**.

**Example**: `a{3}` matches "_aaa_" in "_baaaam_", but not "_aa_" or "_aaaa_".
