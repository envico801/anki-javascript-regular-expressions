========== Question ==========  

### What does `x{n,}` match in a regular expression?  

========== Answer ==========  

**Matches '_n_' or more occurrences of '_x_'**.

**Example**: `a{2,}` matches "_aa_", "_aaa_", "_aaaa_", etc., but not "_a_".
