========== Question ==========  

### What does `x{n,m}` match in a regular expression?  

========== Answer ==========  

**Matches between '_n_' and '_m_' occurrences of '_x_'**.

**Example**: `a{2,4}` matches "_aa_", "_aaa_", and "_aaaa_", but not "_a_" or "_aaaaa_".
