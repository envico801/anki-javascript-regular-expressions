========== Question ==========  

### What does `(?<Name>x)` represent in regular expressions?  

========== Answer ==========  

Named capturing group. Matches and stores the content under a specified name.

**Example**: `(?<year>\d{4})-(?<month>\d{2})-(?<day>\d{2})` captures date parts.
