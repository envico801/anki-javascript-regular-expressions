========== Question ==========  

### What does `(?<=y)x` represent in regular expressions?  

========== Answer ==========  

Positive lookbehind. Matches 'x' only if it's preceded by 'y'.

Example: `(?<=Jack)Sprat` matches "Sprat" in "JackSprat" but not in "TomSprat".
