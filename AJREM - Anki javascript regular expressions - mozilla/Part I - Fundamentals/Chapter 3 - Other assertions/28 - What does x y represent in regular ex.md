========== Question ==========  

### What does `x(?=y)` represent in regular expressions?  

========== Answer ==========  

Positive lookahead. Matches 'x' only if it's followed by 'y'.

**Example**: `Jack(?=Sprat)` matches "Jack" in "JackSprat" but not in "JackFrost".
