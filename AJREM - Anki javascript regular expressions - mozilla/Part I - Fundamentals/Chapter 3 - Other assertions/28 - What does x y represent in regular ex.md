========== Question ==========  

### What does `x(?=y)` represent in regular expressions?  

========== Answer ==========  

Positive lookahead. **Matches '_x_' only if it's followed by '_y_'**.

**Example**: `Jack(?=Sprat)` matches "_Jack_" in "_JackSprat_" but not in "_JackFrost_".
