========== Question ==========  

### What does `x(?!y)` represent in regular expressions?  

========== Answer ==========  

Negative lookahead. **Matches '_x_' only if it's not followed by '_y_'**.

**Example**: `Jack(?!Sprat)` matches "_Jack_" in "_JackFrost_" but not in "_JackSprat_".
