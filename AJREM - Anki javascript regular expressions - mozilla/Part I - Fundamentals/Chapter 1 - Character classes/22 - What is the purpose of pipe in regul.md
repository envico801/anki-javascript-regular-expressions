========== Question ==========  

### What is the purpose of `|` (pipe) in regular expressions?  

========== Answer ==========  

**Creates a disjunction, matching either of the alternatives it separates**.

**Example**: `cat|dog` matches either "_cat_" or "_dog_" in a string.
