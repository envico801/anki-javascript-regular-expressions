Q: What does `\W` represent in regular expressions?  
A: Matches any character that is not a word character from the basic Latin alphabet, equivalent to `[^A-Za-z0-9_]`.  
For example, `/\W/` or `/[^A-Za-z0-9_]/` matches "%" in "50%".
