========== Question ==========  

### What does `/<.*>/` match in regular expressions?  

========== Answer ==========  

Greedily matches everything between the first '<' and the last '>' in a string.

Example: `/<.*>/` matches "<foo><bar>" in "start <foo><bar> end".
